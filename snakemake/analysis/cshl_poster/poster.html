Takeaways

  • For gLMs, what you train on outranks how big you train: a 6M region-specific model beats a 40B whole-genome model on promoters.
  • Balanced sampling is the cheap way to get region-specific behaviour without paying for a separate model per region.
  • Region × timescale interacts: promoters favour mammals; CDS favours animals. Treat the training corpus as a curation decision, not a scale-up decision.
  • Future work: principled timescale selection, projection-based curation, cross-species transfer.
